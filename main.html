Paris, France


Paris, often called "The City of Lights," is known for its iconic landmarks, world-class art museums, and rich history. Whether you're gazing at the Eiffel Tower or strolling along the Seine, Paris offers something for everyone.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to thousands of works of art, including the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre - A charming neighborhood with cobblestone streets and artist studios.
  5. Champs-Élysées - One of the most famous avenues in the world, perfect for shopping and sightseeing.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant - A buttery and flaky pastry that is a breakfast favorite in Paris.
  • Escargot - A French delicacy, snails cooked in garlic butter and herbs.
  • Macaron - A colorful, sweet, and delicate meringue-based cookie.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes that can be filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette - A classic French bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.
