Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its stunning architecture, rich culture, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower. It is a city of love, art, history, and food. Whether you're exploring the Louvre Museum or enjoying a café on the Champs-Élysées, Paris offers an unforgettable experience.

  1. Eiffel Tower - The symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, rich in history.
  4. Montmartre - A beautiful neighborhood with artists' studios and stunning views.
  5. Champs-Élysées - A world-famous avenue lined with shops, theaters, and cafés.
  • Croissant - A flaky, buttery pastry, perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot - Snails cooked in garlic and herb butter, a French delicacy.
  • Macaron - Colorful meringue-based cookies with a creamy filling.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes served with various sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette - A classic French bread with a crunchy crust.
